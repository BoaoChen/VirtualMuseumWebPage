3D Model: Standing Bronze Figure

Historical Significance

Among the numerous bronze statues at Sanxingdui, the Grand Standing Figure undoubtedly stands as the supreme leader of the assembly. In terms of attire, appearance, and scale, this statue is considered the "leader" of the group. On a global scale, the Sanxingdui bronze Grand Standing Figure is also the largest bronze human statue from its period. The statue was cast using a segmented casting technique, resulting in a hollow body composed of two parts: the figure and the base. Its overall image is dignified and solemn, seemingly depicting a powerful figure with extraordinary abilities performing a ritual. The square platform on which it stands can be understood as the ritual site — a sacred altar or divine mountain.

Dynasty

Chinese Historical Era, Shang (1600 BC ~ 1046 BC)
